ABOUT US

LAC an event management company, was created with the intention of exploring the depths of human imaginations through well-defined concept based music gigs and festivals. We are solely dedicated towards making passionate concept based musical gigs where the audience takes home much more than a normal musical experience. We want to tell different stories to the audience with music as its prime vehicle. We aim to bring all different music lovers beyond age in one platform to enjoy, relish, forget and realize the journeys beyond physical exhaustion. We are set to use science, arts and exploration as tools to showcase different physical and meta-physical realms.
